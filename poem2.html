Spring- The budding Hope

Nature sheds its gloomy appearance

Bare trees, empty nests, frosty weather

All replaced with a lovely blooming spring.

Magical vision of budding flowers,

Chirpy birds, friendly people

Golden sunlight, scented breeze.


It is time to shed all those negativities-

Of dead yesterdays, the worries

Fear, uncertainty and empty feelings.

Fill the void with sweet memories

Let go off the anger and frustration

Though the heart ache will stay for a lifetime.


The dear lives taken away untimely,

Surrounds you with clouds of sadness

Bringing back tides of loving memories

Hold on to the happy moments.


Joy of togetherness

Strolls to the park

Walking hand in hand,

Candlelit dinners

Trips to the beach,

All that chatter and laughter.


Times of anger and anguish

Broken promises

Denied love, strong emotions,

Heated arguments that leave you

Heartbroken with teary eyes and misty vision.


Remember we are only mere humans

Full of imperfections.

Your loved ones will be watching over you,

Just keep the Hope candle burring

Because the world will keep on turning

Till the meet up on that beautiful shore.


Dedicated to all those who lost their dear ones to Covid 19 pandemic, special mention to health care workers, all key worker’s, and families of overseas workers who is didn’t get to say goodbye and still struggling to come to terms with their loss.
