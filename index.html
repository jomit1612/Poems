About me

Born and raised in southern India in the beautiful state of Kerala, I developed a love for the arts and creative expression at a young age. After completing my nursing education in Bombay, I gained valuable experience working in Kuwait for several years.

Now residing in the UK, I continue to pursue my passion for writing through the creation of poems. My poems explore themes of love, life, and humanity, touching on both the light and the dark aspects of the human experience.

I am excited to share my work on this website and connect with like-minded individuals who appreciate the power of words. Thank you for visiting and I hope my poems bring you a sense of joy and reflection.
